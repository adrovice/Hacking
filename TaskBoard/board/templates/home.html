Task Board

Welcome to the Task Board! The Task Board is a central tool for organizing and managing tasks for our team.

How does the board work?

  • To Do: This is where new tasks are created and placed that still need to be worked on.
  • In Progress: Tasks assigned to a team member and currently being worked on are located in this section.
  • Review: After a task is completed, it is placed here for review by other team members.
  • Done: Tasks that have been successfully completed are placed here.
